Send your rent, lease or ownership details to the Valuation Office Agency

If you rent, lease or own a business property, you need to send the Valuation Office Agency (VOA) your details if they’ve asked you by letter to do so.

You’ll need the following information to use this service:

  • your lease or agreement details (or any tenant’s details if you’re the owner)
  • details of any sub-lettings
  • details of any improvements or changes you’ve made to the property
  • details of your rent, including the date your rent was fixed
  • the name and address of your landlord, if you're a tenant
  • any rent review periods
  • any rent-free periods or other incentives

You could be taken to court if you submit false information.

Start now

Before you start

It takes about 20 mins to use this service.

You don’t have to complete it in one go. You can save what you’ve done and return to finish later on.

If you need help

Contact the VOA team in your area for help with using the service.

You can find their contact details on the letter sent by the VOA.
